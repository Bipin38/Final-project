Tadasana

Tāḍāsana is from the Sanskrit words ताड tāḍa, "mountain" and आसन āsana meaning "posture" or "seat"

How to do it:

  1. Stand with the feet together or about 10 cm apart, and the arms by the sides.
  2. Raise the arms over the head.
  3. Interlock the fingers and turn the palms upward.
  4. Fix the eyes at a point on the wall slightly above the level of the head.
  5. Inhale and stretch the arms, shoulders and chest upward. Raise the heels, coming up on to the toes
  6. Stretch the whole body from top to bottom, without losing balance or moving the feet.
  7. Hold the breath and the position for a few seconds.
